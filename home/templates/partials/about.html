Ignyt - The Beer Longue is more than just a venue

A great destination for beer enthusiasts seeking an extraordinary experience located at Kumarswamy Layout, South Banglore having an operational hours from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM.

  • Serves variety of alcohol.
  • Also serves both veg and non-veg dishes.
  • Dishes like Creamy Mushroom, Chicken Biryani are some of the must-try dishes here.

The Beer Station plays music. It's a great combination of good drinks and awesome entertainment. Nothing beats enjoying a cold beer while listening to some live tunes. It offers great atmosphere and the perfect spot to hangout with friends. There's a convinient parking available when you're heading out for a night of fun. So, you can have worry-free experience and indulge in some delicious beers and live music without any parking hassels!
